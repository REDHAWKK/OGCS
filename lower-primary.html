Welcome to Lower Primary (1–3)

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and creativity for pupils aged 6–8.

About Primary 1–3

Lower Primary is where children transition from playful exploration into more structured learning. Our pupils, aged 6–8, are guided to build confidence in reading, writing, and problem-solving.

With engaging lessons, supportive teachers, and plenty of creativity, pupils develop skills that prepare them for lifelong success.
