Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions and enrollment.

Are the School fees affordable?

Yes, school and bus fees are affordable and considerable

Is there an entrance exam?

For any class, the child will take a test to determine if he/she is fit for the class.

Do you offer after-school care?

Yes, we offer extended care until 6:00 PM for an additional fee. This includes homework help, supervised play, and snack time.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Yes, we accept mid-year transfers. The application process is the same, and we work closely with transferring students to ensure a smooth transition.
