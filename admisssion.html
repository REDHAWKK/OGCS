Admission Process

Our straightforward process ensures a smooth transition for your child into our school community.

Begin by submitting an inquiry form or scheduling a school tour to experience our environment firsthand.

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, etc.).

Assessment & Interview

Students participate in an age-appropriate assessment, followed by a family interview with our admissions team.

Receive notification of admission status within two weeks of completing all steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions and enrollment.

What is the application deadline?

We accept applications on a rolling basis, but priority is given to applications received by March 1st for the following academic year. Some grades may have limited availability later in the year.

Is there an entrance exam?

For Early Years (ages 2-5), we conduct developmental readiness assessments rather than exams. For Elementary and Middle School, students complete age-appropriate assessments in core subjects to help us understand their learning needs.

Do you offer after-school care?

Yes, we offer extended care until 6:00 PM for an additional fee. This includes homework help, supervised play, and snack time.

What is your student-to-teacher ratio?

Our average class size is 18 students with a student-to-teacher ratio of 8:1 in Early Years and 12:1 in Elementary and Middle School.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Yes, we consider mid-year transfers based on availability. The application process is the same, and we work closely with transferring students to ensure a smooth transition.
