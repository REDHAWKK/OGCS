Nursery Classes

Building strong foundations through play, discovery, and early learning.

Nursery 1

Nursery 1 is the beginning of your child’s structured learning journey. Through guided play, songs, and storytelling, children develop their first skills in communication, creativity, and social interaction.

  • Introduction to letters, numbers, colors, and shapes
  • Focus on social and emotional development
  • Hands-on activities to encourage creativity
  • A warm and supportive environment

Nursery 2

Nursery 2 prepares children for Kindergarten with a balance of fun and structured learning. Early literacy and numeracy skills are developed while still encouraging imagination and discovery.

  • Pre-reading and pre-writing skill development
  • Basic number operations and problem-solving
  • Group projects to build confidence and teamwork
  • Creative arts, music, and outdoor play
